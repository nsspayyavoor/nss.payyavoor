Services

What We Do.

The NSS Unit No. 18 of Sacred Heart Higher Secondary School, Payyavoor, is committed to community service, social welfare, and personal development. We aim to empower students to be responsible citizens through various outreach programs and initiatives that create a lasting positive impact on society.

Community Outreach

Engaging in activities like cleanliness drives, awareness campaigns, and social welfare programs to uplift local communities.

Skill Development

Organizing workshops and training sessions to enhance leadership, teamwork, and life skills among students.

Environmental Initiatives

Promoting eco-friendly practices through tree planting, waste management, and sustainability projects.

Social Awareness

Conducting campaigns on health, education, and social issues to foster a more informed and responsible society.
